Perfect Plate Case Study

We were challenged to create an original app design that solves for an everyday problem. Immediately, our minds went to helping people in the kitchen. To determine that were was a need for such an app and to solidify our direction, we set up 12 user interviews to ask about their hopes, goals, and struggles in the kitchen.
Through our user research we found that most of our users are everyday home chefs, juggling busy schedules but committed to serving up wholesome, budget-friendly meals for themselves and their families. Their kitchen woes revolve around common frustrations: overcooking or under-cooking dishes, dealing with food waste, and the overall hassle it can cause

We believe that users should have the confidence that their meals will turn out perfectly on the first try. Recognizing that not everyone has the time or resources to risk preparing food improperly, especially when their culinary skills are still developing, we propose the development of an app.We recognize that several factors effect the outcome of a meal like:

  • Altitude
  • Appliance used
  • Type of pot, pan, or dish used
  • Amount of food
  • Type of fat used

Our app would gather all of this information to provide foolproof and personalized instructions; assisting people in preparing their meals flawlessly.

Based on valuable insights from user interviews, we tailored our features to address their kitchen needs and aspirations:
  • Altitude Detection: We use location services to determine altitude ensuring precise cooking instructions.
  • My Kitchen: Upon signing up, users can effortlessly build their digital kitchen inventory by adding all their cooking tools, which they can update anytime.
  • Start a New Meal: Users can input their choice of protein or produce, specify quantities, and preferred cooking methods. Our app then generates foolproof cooking instructions.
  • Pro-Tip Feature: To inspire creativity and elevate cooking skills, each set of instructions includes a 'Pro-Tip' to take dishes to the next level.
  • Past Meals: All previously cooked meals are saved for quick and easy access, ensuring users always have their go-to recipes at hand.
Following usability testing of our initial app version, we received a significant piece of feedback: the micro copy was too formal and serious for users' tastes. They were seeking a more relaxed and friendly tone that better aligns with the joyful nature of cooking. In response, we iterated to infuse the app with a more casual and playful language to enhance the user experience
